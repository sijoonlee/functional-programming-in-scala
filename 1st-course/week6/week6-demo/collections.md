## Range
- represented as single object with three fields
    - lower bound, upper bound, step value
- until(exclusive)
    - val r:Range = 1 until 5 // 1,2,3,4
- to(inclusive)
    - val s:Range = 1 to 5 // 1,2,3,4,5
- by(step)
    - val t:Range = 1 to 10 by 3 // 1, 4, 7, 10
